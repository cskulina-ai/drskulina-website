About

A Commitment to Expert, Patient-Centred Neurological Care

Dr Christian Skulina is a Consultant Neurologist and Neurophysiologist with extensive experience in the diagnosis and management of complex neurological conditions. He practises at the intersection of clinical neurology and procedural medicine, offering patients access to highly specialised injection therapies delivered with precision and care.

Dr Skulina consults at Northern Neuroscience and Northern Beaches Neurology, serving patients across Sydney's North Shore and Northern Beaches. He has developed particular expertise in specialist injection therapies — including EMG-guided procedures — for a wide range of neurological and cosmetic conditions. His approach combines rigorous clinical assessment with the latest evidence-based treatments, ensuring each patient receives a personalised management plan.

FRACP — Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Physicians

Areas of Expertise

Specialist Neurological Services

Dr Skulina offers comprehensive consultation and treatment across a broad range of neurological and neurophysiological conditions, with a particular focus on injection-based therapies.

Migraine & Chronic Headache

Advanced management of chronic migraine and complex headache disorders, including preventive injectable therapy for treatment-refractory cases where clinically appropriate, in line with current evidence-based guidelines.

  • Chronic migraine assessment
  • Preventive injectable therapy
  • Cluster headache management
  • Medication overuse headache

Facial Spasms & TMJ Disorders

Specialist diagnosis and treatment of hemifacial spasm, blepharospasm, and temporomandibular joint disorders using targeted injection therapies where clinically appropriate to relieve pain and abnormal muscle activity.

  • Hemifacial spasm
  • Blepharospasm
  • TMJ pain and bruxism
  • Oromandibular dystonia

Dystonia & Spasticity

Expert management of focal and generalised dystonia and spasticity arising from stroke, cerebral palsy, multiple sclerosis and other neurological conditions, using EMG-guided injection therapies where clinically appropriate.

  • Cervical dystonia (torticollis)
  • Writer's cramp & focal dystonias
  • Post-stroke spasticity
  • Upper and lower limb spasticity

Chronic Neck Pain

Targeted assessment and injection therapy for chronic neck pain conditions, including myofascial pain syndrome and cervicogenic headache, using EMG-guided precision injection therapies.

  • Cervicogenic headache
  • Myofascial pain syndrome
  • Whiplash-associated disorders
  • EMG-guided injection therapy

EMG-Guided Injections

Electromyography (EMG) guidance ensures precise needle placement within target muscles, optimising treatment outcomes while minimising risk. This technique is particularly valuable for deep or small muscle groups.

  • Real-time EMG monitoring
  • Precision muscle targeting
  • Reduced risk of complications
  • Optimised dosing & outcomes

Hyperhidrosis

Comprehensive assessment and management of primary hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating) using injectable therapies where clinically appropriate — a highly effective, evidence-based treatment approach for focal hyperhidrosis affecting the underarms, hands, feet, and face.

  • Axillary (underarm) hyperhidrosis
  • Palmar (hand) hyperhidrosis
  • Plantar (foot) hyperhidrosis
  • Facial & gustatory sweating

Non-Surgical Cosmetic Procedures

Drawing on his deep expertise in facial musculature and neurological precision, Dr Skulina also offers non-surgical cosmetic procedures — delivering natural, refined results underpinned by a thorough medical understanding of facial anatomy. Cosmetic appointments are available in Mosman.

  • Forehead & frown lines
  • Crow's feet
  • Brow lifting
  • Jaw slimming (masseter)
